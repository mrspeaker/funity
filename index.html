Tutorial
========

1. Click "New Game" to start fresh
2. Click "Add Entity" to add a new entity
3. In the "Renderer" component, click "select" to choose an alternative image
4. Select "Key Controller" from the "component:" dropdown box
5. Click "add comp" to add the component to the entity.
6. Press "Play" and move around the screen with arrow keys!
7. Cange the "speed" in the "Key Controller" component to go faster

Then try duplicating the entity. Or add these components: Wander (wanders randomly around the screen),
ColorChange (changes the background color randomly), MoveSine (moves in a sine motion).
Have a look at how the demo game works (/game/scene1.js)
